About Us

Tech Palewi is a non-governmental organization (a.k.a. called civil society organization), born in 1998 and its name comes from Nahuatl and means: “I will look after you, I will help you grow”. We are a growing organization that seeks to contribute to the strengthening, social, and human development of all people and ethnicities.
